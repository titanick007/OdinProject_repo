Do I know guitar???!!


Yes!! I do know how to play the guitar. My level would be around intermediate.
I started learning the guitar because the worship leader in my church wanted to teach a few of the kids in the church.
So I started learning in 6th grade. I learnt the basics within about 6 months to a year.
After that I stagnated because my parents never forced me to go to any guitar lessons or anything.
But around 11th grade my dad bought me an electric guitar because of my good grades in 10th grade.
That really motivated me to take my guitar skills to another level.
